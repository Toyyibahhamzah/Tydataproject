NESTLE

As a Data Analyst in Nestlé's Growth and Strategy Department, using Power BI, I accessed the Nestlé dataset and conducted thorough data cleaning and preparation using Power Query. Subsequently, I leveraged DAX to perform advanced calculations and developed a product comparison dashboard to aid expansion plans. This report summarizes key findings and recommendations based on the analysis conducted. Nescafe showed consistent growth, while Kit Kat remained stable. Nes Cau declined in 2020, highlighting areas for improvement. Seasonal patterns were evident, with June and July sales peaks and lower December figures. Products like Nes Cau and Maggi were significant revenue generators, while Nesquik Duo and Smarties yielded lower revenues. Tasmania reported the highest sales, while Western Australia had the lowest, indicating regional disparities. 77% of revenue came from direct sales, with 23% from online sales, highlighting the importance of both channels. Diversify product lines, capitalize on seasonal trends, target regional markets, expand e-commerce initiatives, and implement continuous monitoring for sustained growth.

RAY GADGETS ELECTRONICS

Ray Gadgets Electronics, a pioneering force in the electronics industry, continues to shape the market landscape through innovation and reliability. The company embarked on a comprehensive Power BI project aimed at conducting a profitability analysis spanning from 2015 onwards. The primary goal of this initiative is to streamline profitability analysis, particularly focusing on diagnosing the performance of the month of May. Key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total transactions, quantity sold, revenue, profit, and profit margin serve as crucial metrics meticulously tracked and visualized to empower stakeholders with actionable insights. The project features a robust data model structured around a star schema, comprising one fact table and five dimension tables, including a Calendar table optimized through DAX functions. Relationships between tables are established to facilitate seamless data analysis. Across three distinct pages—Summary, Diagnosing May, and Profitability Analysis—the Power BI report canvas offers visually compelling and insightful visualizations. Key metrics such as total revenue, total profit, profit margin, and product/category-wise performance metrics are prominently featured to provide a comprehensive understanding of the company's financial health. Insights gleaned from the analysis highlight significant achievements, including selling over 11 million products and amassing $7 billion in revenue, with a commendable 33% profit margin. Product profitability analysis underscores the dominance of smartphones, laptops, and digital cameras. Customer loyalty is showcased through the identification of the top 5 most loyal customers. May's performance analysis reveals a 25% decline in total profit compared to April, with variations observed across product categories. Notably, despite the decline in total profit, the company maintains a strong profit margin of over 33%, indicative of operational efficiency. Recommendations derived from the analysis include focusing marketing efforts on flagship products, nurturing relationships with top customers, and exploring strategies to enhance May performance. Key metrics such as total revenue, profit margin, quantity sold, and product/category-wise performance metrics guide these strategic decisions. The seamless deployment of the Power BI report to the Power BI Service underscores the company's commitment to leveraging technology for informed decision-making and sustained market leadership. This integration ensures convenient access and collaboration with management, further enhancing the company's analytical capabilities.

HOSPITAL TRANSACTION

In a recent project, I conducted a thorough analysis of a hospital's financial and operational performance over three years (2021-2023) using Power BI dashboards. The objective was to uncover valuable insights focusing on metrics like revenue, expenses, profitability, doctor performance, and patient demographics. Through meticulous data preprocessing using Power Query, creating a fact table and 5 dimension tables and the formulation of key performance indicators (KPIs) using DAX, I uncovered significant trends and insights. The analysis revealed that the hospital generated $274,000 in revenue with a profit margin of 31%. Intriguing trends included fluctuations in revenue and spikes in patient visits across different quarters. Loyalty among initial patients was consistent, but 2023 saw a decline in patient turnout, prompting further investigation into potential factors like service quality. Specialty-wise analysis highlighted higher patient volumes in Cardiology, Neurology, and Dermatology departments. Recommendations included delving deeper into specialty profitability, addressing factors contributing to revenue decline, and recognizing top-performing medical practitioners to enhance overall performance. This project underscores the value of data analytics in optimizing healthcare performance and decision-making.

Loan Portfolio Risk Assessment for Daiz Bank

## Daiz Bank Loan Portfolio: Risk Factors and Improvement Daiz Bank's loan portfolio analysis revealed areas for concern. While repayment occurs in 62% of cases, a significant portion (21% delinquent, 17% defaulted) indicates risk. Borrower demographics (age, marital status, employment) and loan characteristics (amount, term) influence loan applications and risk. This report recommends improvements in loan approval processes, early warning systems for defaults, portfolio diversification, and customer support programs to mitigate risk and ensure financial stability.
