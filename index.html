Redox Airline Project

As a consultant for a small airline in Chicago, I addressed various challenges by identifying issues with management's finance and data analysis proficiency, including outdated data architecture. My contributions aimed to inform strategic decisions and enhance the airline's success. Utilizing Excel's Index Match function for data consolidation and considering data model relationships, I conducted revenue analysis, highlighting REDOX Airline's cost-effectiveness and crucial factors affecting profitability such as overhead expenses and route efficiency. Key findings included the dominance of the B737 aircraft in the fleet, which accounted for over 46% of flights and generated the highest revenue but also experienced the longest departure delays. The A319 and A320 followed in terms of revenue generation and performance, respectively. Analysis showed that the ORD-LAX route played a crucial role in driving overall revenue, contributing significantly to the airline's $531,225,803 total revenue. Despite a surge in flights in February, the airline experienced a decline in October, with potential areas for enhancement. Metrics revealed disparities in profitability among airports, with LAX generating the highest revenue of $51,138,857, while SLC fell behind with only $2,165,882. Furthermore, certain routes, such as ORD-DCA, proved exceptionally lucrative, generating $1,672 per mile, while others like ORD-LAX exhibited lower profitability at $0.48 per mile. Analysis also showed a 1% increase in revenue between 2014 and 2015, with a total of 4,822 flights and revenue of $269,357,727, indicating potential for improvement. Strategies recommended for optimization included evaluating route efficiency, capitalizing on seasonal demand fluctuations, and optimizing aircraft utilization and profitability. Monitoring and analyzing data consistently are crucial for staying abreast of travel trends and adjusting strategies accordingly. .

MEKKO RETAIL INC

I collaborated closely with MEKKO Retail Inc. to conceptualize and develop an Excel-based interactive dashboard tailored to their unique needs. This comprehensive tool, crafted through my efforts, meticulously aggregates and visualizes key performance indicators (KPIs), including sales revenue, customer footfall, and average transaction value, alongside essential demographic metrics such as age distribution, geographic origin, and purchasing preferences. By providing MEKKO with real-time access to actionable data, my solution empowers them to make informed decisions swiftly, adapt to market dynamics effectively, and maintain a competitive edge. Through my in-depth analysis, I identified core demographics, with age groups 17-27 and 28-37 emerging as primary targets, while also pinpointing top-performing departments such as clothing, electronics, and jewelry based on metrics like sales growth and customer engagement. Moreover, my insights uncovered untapped market segments, such as customers with vehicles other than Toyota and Ford, presenting opportunities for targeted marketing strategies. With a focus on growth potential, my recommendations include crafting tailored marketing campaigns, strategically expanding into high-growth markets identified through metrics such as customer acquisition rates and market penetration, leveraging customer segmentation to personalize offerings, and embracing omnichannel marketing strategies to enhance the overall customer experience across various touchpoints.
